# Privacy Policy — Textfiles Reader

*Last updated: April 2026*

Textfiles Reader does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## What the app does

- Fetches publicly available text files from textfiles.com for playback
- Stores your play history locally on your device using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults)
- Uses iOS text-to-speech (AVSpeechSynthesizer) for audio playback — all processing happens on-device

## What the app does not do

- No user accounts or registration
- No analytics or crash reporting
- No advertising or tracking
- No data sent to any server other than textfiles.com to fetch text files
- Play history never leaves your device

## Third-party services

The app fetches content from textfiles.com and similar 3rd-party public sites. Those site's own privacy policy governs any logging they may do of HTTP requests.
